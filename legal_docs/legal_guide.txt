Vehicle Theft:
Vehicle theft is the unlawful taking of a motor vehicle without the owner's consent.
Punishment may include imprisonment and fines according to applicable laws.

Cyber Crime:
Cyber crime includes hacking, identity theft, phishing, online fraud, and unauthorized access to digital systems.
Punishment depends on severity and applicable cyber laws.

Fraud:
Fraud involves deception for personal or financial gain.
Punishment may include imprisonment and monetary penalties.

Assault:
Assault refers to intentionally causing physical harm or threatening harm to another person.
Punishment varies based on severity.

Property Damage:
Property damage involves intentionally damaging another person's property.
Punishment may include fines and imprisonment.

Online Harassment:
Online harassment includes repeated threatening, abusive, or offensive communication through digital platforms.
Victims may report incidents to cyber crime authorities.

Identity Theft:
Identity theft occurs when someone unlawfully uses another person's personal information for fraudulent purposes.
